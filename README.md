## 📌 Featured Projects

### 🔴 [HR Attrition Analysis](https://github.com/Jeetup-co/hr-attrition-analysis-powerbi)
> Analysed IBM HR dataset of **1,470 employees** to uncover key drivers of employee turnover.

- Overall attrition rate: **16.12%**
- Overtime workers leave at **3× the rate** of non-overtime employees
- Sales department had the highest attrition at **20.63%**
- Built interactive **Power BI dashboard** with SQL (SQLite3) + Python EDA
- **Tools:** Python · Pandas · SQLite3 · Matplotlib · Seaborn · Power BI

---

### 🟠 [Amazon Sales Analysis](https://github.com/Jeetup-co/Amazon_sales_analysis)
> Business performance analysis of **1,465 Amazon product records** to identify revenue drivers and discount strategy insights.

- Cleaned and transformed real-world e-commerce data (price symbols, nulls, categories)
- Engineered features: discount bands, price ranges, rating buckets, net savings %
- Identified top-performing product categories and high-discount impact on ratings
- **Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn
